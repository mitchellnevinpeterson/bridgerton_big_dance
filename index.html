The Big Dance

Stanford's 26th Annual Ball - A Bridgerton-Inspired Garden Party

🗓 Date: Friday, May 9 into Saturday, May 10

🕘 Time: 9 o’clock in the evening – 6 o’clock at dawn

📍 Location: Roble Arts Gym

*Optional Intermediate Cross-Step Waltz lesson at 8 P.M. included with ticket

Prepare to dance beneath cascading garlands, to lose yourself in a symphony of swirling gowns and sly glances, all set to the masterful selections of Richard Powers, our DJ and guide through the evening’s enchantments.

🌟 Important Notice to All Attendees

To preserve the sanctity of our grand ballroom, we entreat you to don only soft-soled dance shoes (you may also wear socks) within Roble Gym. Let your steps be light, your turns be elegant, and your soles be suitably gentle.

🎩 Attire for the Evening

  • Regency Finery – Step into the world of Bridgerton with empire-waist gowns, tailcoats, and enough lace and brocade to make Lady Danbury weep.
  • Floral & Foliage Fantasy – Adorn yourself in blooms, petals, and garden whimsy—clothing kissed by spring itself.
  • Authentic Excellence – Come as you are, for even the Queen knows the rarest jewel is a soul unmasked.

👑 Costume Contest

There shall be a costume contest for those whose ensembles truly dazzle the ton. Whether you appear as a duke, a duchess, a dandy, or a daffodil—prepare to be seen, and perhaps even crowned.

🎟 Tickets

General Public: $20
